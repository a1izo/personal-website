About

I study software engineering at VIA University College. I hold Certiport certifications in Python, databases and device configuration, and I spent two years teaching IT and robotics workshops at ROBOCODE.

Outside class, I edit videos. I cut promos and short form content in After Effects for clients who need raw footage turned into something people actually finish watching. Pairing the two wasn't really a plan, it just happened, and now I split my time between them fairly evenly.

I like having a clear direction and understanding the bigger goal, but I don't wait for a plan to be perfect before starting. I break a problem into smaller steps, work through them, and adjust as I go. In a team I tend to become the person keeping things organized and moving, and I care as much about people feeling comfortable contributing as I do about the technical work itself. Learn by doing, communicate clearly, take ownership until the job is actually done, that's roughly how I work.

Right now I'm looking for a part time student job. Tech work would be ideal, but I'm open to any office role that keeps me busy.
